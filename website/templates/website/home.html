How It Works

Pasnevesht allows you to securely create scheduled messages that will be delivered automatically. You choose the recipient, the delivery condition, and we handle the rest.

1. Create Your Message

Write your message and choose the recipient's email address.

2. Set Delivery Conditions

Select a specific date or define inactivity duration.

3. Automatic Delivery

The system securely sends your message when conditions are met.
